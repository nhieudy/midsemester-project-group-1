Welcome to the World of Jurassic Park

"An adventure 65 million years in the making..."

The Jurassic Park franchise is a series of books, films and video games centering on the attempt to create a theme park of cloned dinosaurs. It began in 1990 when Universal Studios bought the rights to the novel by Michael Crichton before it was even published. The book was very successful, as was the 1993 film adaptation which led to five sequels, although the third, fourth, fifth, sixth, and seventh films were not based on a novel, as the previous two films were. The software developers Ocean Software, BlueSky Software and Sega of America have had the rights to developing video games since the 1993 film, and numerous games have been produced.
